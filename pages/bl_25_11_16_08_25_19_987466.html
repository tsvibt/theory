That's fine. Whether or not you go around saying "God" is among the least of God's concerns. What matters is:

  • Look at your life through heaven's eyes. When you and your friends and the rest of humanity have grown up for a thousand years, what will they think of what you are doing today? They will be incomprehensibly empathetic--but they will still judge you. How will they judge you? You cannot know. But you can know some aspects of this. Are you choosing life and repairing the world?
  • Do the right actions. If you know the right actions but don't feel the right feelings about them, you still fulfill your obligations by doing the right actions; if you have the right feelings but do not do the right actions, you do not fulfill your obligations.
